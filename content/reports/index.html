Introduction

WAI-Adapt is using "data-*" approach at the moment to gain implementation experience and demonstrate the usefulness of WAI-Adapt in practice. Implementers are cautioned that the "data-" prefix will be removed or replaced for some or all attribute values in succeeding iterations of this specification following additional consideration in W3C and the WHATWG. See implementation information in Explainer.

There are some early implementations of content that can maximize the benefit for users.
